# Repump

Launch tokens with built-in X creator fee sharing. Share fees with up to 10 wallets, authorize via X, and claim earnings seamlessly.

## Overview

Repump is a token launch platform that integrates **creator fee sharing directly with X (Twitter)**.  
Creators can launch tokens and automatically distribute fees to multiple wallets while authenticating through their X account.

Repump enables social-driven token launches where communities and collaborators are rewarded transparently.

---

## Features

- 🚀 **Instant Token Launch**
  Create and launch tokens in seconds.

- 💰 **Creator Fee Sharing**
  Distribute fees across **up to 10 wallets**.

- 🐦 **X Authorization**
  Authenticate securely using your X account.

- 📊 **Transparent Revenue**
  Track and claim earnings seamlessly.

- ⚡ **Simple & Fast**
  Minimal setup, maximum efficiency.

---

## How It Works

1. Connect your wallet.
2. Authorize using your **X account**.
3. Launch your token.
4. Configure **fee sharing (up to 10 wallets)**.
5. Start earning and claim your rewards.
